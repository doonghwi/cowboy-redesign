## 16종 (공통 블록 뒤에 이어 붙이기) — 각 캐릭터의 "포인트"

### 1. 일반인 (commoner) — 포인트: 지극히 평범한 everyman
`plain ordinary rookie cowboy, the everyman — deliberately average and a little nervous, simple worn vest, modest unremarkable look, warm tan-brown accents.`

### 2. 준비자 (prepper) — 포인트: 강박적으로 만반의 준비
`over-prepared cowboy obsessively loaded with gear, bandoliers and pouches and spare bullets everywhere, hyper-alert ready expression, golden-yellow accents.`

### 3. 스나이퍼 (sniper) — 포인트: 외눈의 날카로운 집중
`ice-cold sniper defined by one piercing narrowed eye and intense focus, scar across the brow, steady unblinking stare down a scoped rifle, crimson-red accents.`

### 4. 스피드로더 (speedloader) — 포인트: 잔상이 남는 빠른 손
`hyper-fast gunslinger with blurred restless hands spinning a revolver, twitchy energetic grin, fingerless gloves, rust-orange accents.`

### 5. 의사 (doctor) — 포인트: 지쳤지만 다정한 야전 의사
`weary frontier medic, tired kind eyes behind round glasses, blood-stained coat and stethoscope, exhausted but caring, teal-green accents.`

### 6. 스모커 (smoker) — 포인트: 연기 너머 무심함
`aloof scruffy cowboy squinting through cigarillo smoke, indifferent half-lidded gaze, unbothered slouch, slate blue-gray accents.`

### 7. 사냥꾼 (hunter) — 포인트: 야성적인 추적자
`feral rugged tracker with scars and an unkempt beard, fur coat, sharp predatory eyes, a steel bear-trap on the belt, dark-brown accents.`

### 8. 리셋터 (resetter) — 포인트: 섬뜩하게 차분한 시간 조작자
`unnervingly calm time-manipulator, blank emotionless face, neat and precise, faint glowing clock-rune motif, steel-blue accents.`

### 9. 결투가 (duelist) — 포인트: 거만한 귀족풍 결투가
`arrogant aristocratic duelist with a proud smirk and sharp features, flamboyant flowing scarf, two revolvers, deep-purple accents.`

### 10. 평화주의자 (pacifist) — 포인트: 수도승 같은 온화함
`gentle serene pacifist with soft warm eyes and an almost monk-like calm, gun wrapped in white cloth, holding a desert flower, sage-green accents.`

### 11. 그림자 (shadow) — 포인트: 읽을 수 없는 은폐
`secretive cowboy whose face stays in shadow under a low hat brim, only sharp glinting eyes visible, unreadable mysterious aura, near-black accents.`

### 12. 러시안룰렛 (roulette) — 포인트: 광기 어린 도박꾼
`unhinged manic gambler with a wild crazed grin and reckless eyes, open revolver cylinder showing one bullet, blood-red accents.`

### 13. 쌍권총 (dualgun) — 포인트: 떠들썩한 쇼맨
`boisterous showman dual-wielding two revolvers, big confident laugh, flashy energetic pose, fiery orange-brown accents.`

### 14. 파파라치 (paparazzi) — 포인트: 캐는 거 좋아하는 능청
`nosy sneaky cowboy peeking with a vintage flash camera, mischievous sly grin, press-pass tucked in the hat, bright blue accents.`

### 15. 부두술사 (voodoo) — 포인트: 으스스한 주술사
`creepy occult witch-doctor with skull face-paint and an unsettling grin, holding a cursed doll, glowing purple hex sigils, deep-purple accents.`
